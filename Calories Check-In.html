Calories Check-In

^ Click the "Meal Entry Form" ^

    STEPS:

    Click on the "..." on the top right, then click "Make a Copy." This is where you will be able to access your responses later on.

    To access the form, you will fill it with everything you consume throughout the day; click on the eye icon on the top right of the screen.

    Fill out the form with a food/drink you have in the day and the number of calories in it, and submit it.

    Click "submit another response" every time you eat or drink something else.

    At the end of the day, once you are done filling out the form with all the food/drinks you've eaten throughout the day, to access all your responses, go back to the copy of the form you initially made.

    Click on "Responses" at the top center.

    Click on the green icon on the top right of the screen that says "Create a Spreadsheet," and choose to create a new spreadsheet.

    You can access your entries throughout the day with a time stamp.

    In order to calculate your daily consumption of calories automatically, follow the steps below.

     

    How to Set Up your Spreadsheet:

    These steps may take a few minutes, but once they are already done, you won't have to do them again.

    Add a sheet to the spreadsheet by clicking on the "+" button on the bottom left of the screen.

    In column A, first cell, type in "Date".

     

    Example:

    In column B, second cell, input the function =TO_DATE(int(select cell A2)).This will turn the timestamp into just the date.

    Click on cell B2, hover over and click on the little blue square on the bottom right corner of the cell, drag it all the way down column B (a random date will appear, this can be ignored, as dates are inputted, this date will change).

    In column C, cell 1, type in "Calories".

    In cell C2, type in = , go to the first sheet and select all cells under "Number of Calories".

    Click on cell C2, hover over and click on the little blue square on the bottom right corner of the cell, drag it all the way down column C.

    In column E, cell 1, type in "Date" again.

    In cell E2, type in =UNIQUE(select all cells in column B).

    Click on cell E2, hover over and click on the small blue square on the bottom right corner of the cell, drag it all the way down column E.

    In column F, cell 1, type in "Daily Sum of Calories".

    In cell F2, insert the function =SUMIF(B2:B1000,E2,C2:C1000).

    Click on cell F2, hover over and click on the little blue square on the bottom right corner of the cell, drag it all the way down column F (do not delete the 0's).
