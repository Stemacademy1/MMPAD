High Intensity:

High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

A mix of strenuous exercises with breaks in between the exercises. HIIT is one of the top ways to lower your body fat percentage and cholesterol.

Weight Lifting

Continues burning fat at an efficient rate even after the workout is over and increases your metabolic rate.

______

Mid Intensity:

Cycling

Can undoubtedly burn the same amount of calories as jogging, but it is way lighter and easier on your knees.

Jumping Jacks

A heart pumping exercise that will lower your cholesterol.

______

Low Intensity:

Walking/Jogging

Begin with a walk and each day gradually increase to a jog.This will lower your cholesterol,as well as blood pressure.

If you need something lighter on your body, you can use swimming to lower your cholesterol.
